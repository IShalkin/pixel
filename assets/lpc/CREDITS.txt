Sprite format: each PNG is a 64x64-per-frame LPC sheet. Walk sheets are laid out
as the standard LPC walk rows (up, left, down, right), 9 frames per row. Body
and hair layers share the same grid, so a hair sheet overlays its matching body
sheet frame-for-frame.
